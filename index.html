Tic Tac Toe Game

Player X: 0
Player O: 0
Draw: 0

Rules:

  • Player X and Player O take turns marking spaces on a 3x3 grid.
  • The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
  • If all spaces are filled and no player has achieved a winning combination, the game is a draw.
